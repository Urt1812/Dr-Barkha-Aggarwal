Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful? +

With modern anaesthesia techniques used at our clinic, root canal treatment is completely painless. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the procedure actually is.

How long does a dental implant procedure take? +

The initial implant placement takes about 1–2 hours. The full process including healing and crown placement takes 3–6 months for best long-term results.

At what age can my child get braces? +

We recommend an orthodontic evaluation around age 7, but braces are typically placed between ages 10–14. Adults can also get braces or clear aligners at any age.

How often should I visit the dentist? +

We recommend a dental checkup and cleaning every 6 months for most patients. Those with specific dental issues may need more frequent visits.

Do you offer cashless or insurance-based treatments? +

Please contact us directly to discuss insurance and payment options. We strive to make dental care affordable and offer flexible payment plans.
